Welcome To The Vibe

Utah's Underground Virgin Bar

"The Vibe" is a non-alcoholic bar in Utah that specializes in virgin cocktails. We strive to create bar culture without the uber home. The menu features a wide range of delicious and creative mocktails, made with fresh fruits, herbs, and high-quality syrups and juices. The d√©cor is modern and stylish, with comfortable seating, dim lighting, and a relaxed vibe that creates the perfect setting for catching up with friends or a date night. The Vibe is a great place to unwind and have a good time without the worries of drinking and driving. Open for all ages, it's a family-friendly alternative to traditional bars and a great destination for anyone looking for a fun, alcohol-free experience.

We Cater!!!

Our catering services are designed to make your event a success, no matter the size or occasion. We offer a wide range of menu options to suit any taste and budget, from casual buffet-style cocktails to formal glasses decorated to perfection. Our team of experienced virgin bartenders use only the freshest, high-quality ingredients to create delicious and visually stunning drinks that are sure to impress your guests. We understand that every event is unique, so we offer custom menu planning to ensure that your catering experience is tailored to your specific needs and preferences. We also provide setup, service, and cleanup, so you can relax and enjoy your event. Our dedicated catering coordinator will work closely with you to plan and execute every aspect of your event, from drink selection to special cups and garnishes. Contact us today to discuss your next event and let us handle the drinks so you can focus on enjoying the occasion!
